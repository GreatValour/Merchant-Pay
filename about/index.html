About

Merchant Pay Connect was founded with a simple approach. “To make Payments Simpler” Today, the merchant payment industry is filled with processors that promise the lowest rate and usually give you introductory prices. These practices have made it difficult for small business to trust and stay with one processor as we continue to see rising costs in processing for no reason. Therefore MPC was founded, to ensure that business is ran with fundamental principles that focus on the merchant and not the cost.

At MPC, we pride ourselves at providing quality service with custom made solutions for every merchant. That’s because every merchant is different and no one size fit’s all. Unlike some of our competitors, we work directly with you and provide customer support where you speak with some that can help you through any issues or services. We focus on what counts, and that’s you!

The MPC pledge – We ensure that every merchant is an important contribution to our company and therefore help establish a reliable, secure, fast, smart and effective solution to help our merchants grow. We don’t focus on anything great than each merchant and are always treated as our only merchant. This is what makes us unique to our clients, our capacity to ensure personal service with leading industry technology. We will be there every step of the way from humble beginnings to your franchise expansion. Merchant Pay Connect, payments made simple.
